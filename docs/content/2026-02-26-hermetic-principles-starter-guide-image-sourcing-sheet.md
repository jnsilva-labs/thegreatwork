- British Library digital collections

## Notes from First-Pass Review

- I excluded some attractive Wellcome/Commons astronomy images because they are CC BY 4.0 rather than public domain (still usable later with attribution, but not in this PD-first set).
- Several symbolic files (for example the Franckenberg seal) are low resolution; treat them as motifs, badges, or small callouts rather than large print plates.
- The two manuscript collections (`MS.879`, `MS.23`) are strong anchors because they contain multiple plates, giving flexibility once you begin layout.
